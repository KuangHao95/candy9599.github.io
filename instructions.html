Instructions

Dear participant , please read the instructions before the experiment.

You will be tasked to select specific items with different menus under different conditions.

MARKING MENU:

1. Press and hold left or right button of the mouse in the white block with yellow borders until a marking menu is shown.
2. For menu with single depth, hold and drag your mouse to target word, release when the word you want to select is highlighted.
3. For menu with multiple depth, hold and drag your mouse to a category which contains your target word, repeat the above step and release till the target word is highlighted.
4. After finishing selecting, click next to continue the experiment.

RADIAL MENU::

1. Click the right mouse button to show the radial menu.
2. For menu with single depth, click the left mouse button to select your target word.
3. For menu with multiple depth, click a category which contains your target word, repeat till the target word shows up and click the left mouse button to select your target word.
4. After finishing selecting, click next to continue the experiment.
